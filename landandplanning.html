Land & Planning

 

Land & County Development has specialist experience in design and planning and has established sound relationships with the regions local district councils. It is our philosophy to work closely with the local Planning Departments, involving them as much as possible at the early stages of design. This transforms into an impressive planning success rate, thus maximising the value of the land whilst reducing the level of uncertainty throughout the planning process.
