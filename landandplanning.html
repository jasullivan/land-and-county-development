Land & Planning

 

Land & County Development was founded in 2009 and is based in the north of Essex close to the borders of Hertfordshire and Cambridgeshire. We have an expanding portfolio and so far, have successfully completed a number of developments to a very high standard. The company specialises in designing and building exceptional homes tailored for modern living which is sympathetic to the surrounding environment. We have specialist experience in planning and design and an enthusiasm for timber frame construction.
